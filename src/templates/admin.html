Facilitator Notes

Game Management
  • Use the "Start Next Round" button to advance the game when all teams have submitted decisions
  • Use "Force Advance Round" only when necessary (e.g., if a team is unresponsive)
  • Monitor team submissions and provide assistance as needed
Learning Objectives
  • Strategic Analysis: Teams should apply frameworks like Porter's Five Forces and SWOT
  • Competitive Positioning: Teams should develop clear strategic positioning
  • Resource Allocation: Teams should make trade-offs in resource allocation
  • Market Adaptation: Teams should respond to changing market conditions
  • Long-term Planning: Teams should balance short-term results with long-term sustainability
Debrief Questions
  • What strategic frameworks did your team find most useful?
  • How did your strategic positioning evolve over the course of the game?
  • What were your most successful decisions? What would you do differently?
  • How did you respond to strategic events and market changes?
  • What connections can you draw between this simulation and real-world strategic management?
